# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
as its code of conduct.

## TL;DR

- Be respectful. Disagreement is fine; ad hominems are not.
- Assume good faith on the other side. Most disagreements are misunderstandings.
- Harassment, discrimination, or threats are not tolerated and will result in
  a ban from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code:

- Open a [private security advisory](https://github.com/yufakang0826-hue/walmart-mcp/security/advisories/new)
  (this is the most private channel for this repo), or
- Email the maintainer at the address listed in `package.json` `author`.

Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed within 7 days.

## Enforcement

Maintainers will follow the
[Contributor Covenant enforcement ladder](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines):
correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban, as appropriate to the
severity and pattern of the violation.
